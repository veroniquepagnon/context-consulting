What we do together

A reading of what is really going on

What these situations have in common is a structure: rational arguments are exhausted, yet something deeper is at play. A decision unlocks when the missing context appears.

In our sessions, we go to the heart of your situation. Through the words you choose, what your body conveys, what circulates through your words. I read what is there and help you see it too.

This is not therapy. It is a form of precise discernment that makes visible and analysable what was still unclear. From there, it becomes easier to decide, to act, and to step back.

The method

Context Consulting

The method I developed to read a situation in all its complexity. It combines two types of reading that are rarely associated. A systematic and concrete inquiry, and the reading of what operates in the invisible, like the intangible influences that can nonetheless be determining.

Contextual analysis

Systemic analysis and human dynamics · Relational dynamics and power stakes · Influence systems activated in the situation.

Symbolic readings

Reading through words, narratives, the body · Symbolic lines · Tarot · Akasha

These two levels of reading do not oppose each other: they complement each other.

Context Consulting scans the terrain. Makes visible what is not yet: the blind spots, the levers and the assets available and ready to be strengthened, so you can position yourself with precision and make decisions without regretting them or second-guessing yourself.

Who I am

Véronique Pagnon

I am Véronique Pagnon, founder of the Context Consulting method. For eight years, I have been working with people who want to see clearly in their situation, whatever it may be.

I created this method at a time when life transitions are accelerating and multiplying, to bring together in a coherent approach the tools I know best.

My background over thirty years weaves together journalism, the study of political and social systems, personal accompaniment, and the contextual analysis of situations people are living through.
