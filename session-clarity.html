What we do together

A reading of what is really going on

What these situations share is structure: the rational arguments are exhausted, but something deeper is at stake. A decision unblocks when the missing context comes into view.

During our session, we go to the heart of your situation. Through the words you choose, through what your body carries, through what circulates beneath your words. I read what is there and help you see it too.

This is not therapy. It is a form of precise discernment that makes visible and workable what was still blurred. From there, it becomes easier to make a decision, take action, and gain perspective.

The session works in the present: not what has been, nor the goals ahead, but what is happening now in your situation and your current context.

The session lasts between 90 minutes and 2h, depending on the depth of your situation. There is nothing to prepare: just arrive with what you are living.

Every Clarity Session activates three levers: the blind spots that were blocking the reading of your situation, the forces at play that were not yet visible, and the path that becomes possible. A new direction appears.

The method

Context Consulting

Context Consulting is the method I developed to read a situation in its full complexity. It combines several analytical systems to make visible what isn't yet.

Contextual analysis

Systemic analysis · Human dynamics and stakes · Relational dynamics · Reading of activated influence systems.

Symbolic readings

Symbolic lines · Tarot · Akasha

These two levels of reading don't oppose each other. They complement. What analysis doesn't yet capture, the symbolic reveals. And the other way around.

Who I am

Véronique Pagnon

I am Véronique Pagnon, founder of the Context Consulting method. For eight years, I have been working with people who want to see clearly in their situation, whatever it may be.
